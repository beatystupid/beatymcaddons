Beaty Addons - A Minecraft Mod

The Beaty Addons Minecraft mod is Released!

Update 1.0.0:

Addons:
New Creative mode Tab
Betanium [Can be used to craft Beaties (4 in a square), betanium blocks (9 in a square), all tools, all armor]
Beaty [Can be used to craft Beaty Blocks, can also be crafted with dripstone, black dye, stick, water, white concrete powder] Beaty Blocks [Can make Beaty Villagers, can make the Beaty World Portal]
Beaty Liquid [Water + Beaty]
Beaty Banner Pattern [in loom]
New Advancements
Beaty World [Usually you spawn in a cave, made of beaty blocks and has Beaty Liquid]
Beaty World Portal Lighter [used to light the Beaty World portal, craft with a beaty and a flind & steel]

Minecraft Version: NeoForge 1.21.8
